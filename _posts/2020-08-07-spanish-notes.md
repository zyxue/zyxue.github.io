# 这个，那个，这些，那些

|                              | male     | female  |        |
|------------------------------|----------|---------|--------|
| this                         | este     | esta    |不定冠词|
| that                         | ese      | esa     |不定冠词|
| these                        | estos    | estas   |不定冠词|
| those                        | esos     | esas    |不定冠词|
| that one over there          | aquel    | aquella |不定冠词|
| that one over there (plural) | aquellos | quellas |不定冠词|
| the                          | el       | la      |  定冠词|
| the (plural)                 | los      | las     |  定冠词|
